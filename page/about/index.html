About

Recommend.Games

Recommend.Games strives to recommend the best board games for you. We take the user ratings from BoardGameGeek (BGG), apply some black magic, and present recommendations that suit you. Check out its own About page to learn more about the details!

If you enjoy our work, please check out the Donations page or tell your friends about us.

Analysis Paralysis

This blog takes the data we collect for Recommend.Games and tries to answer insteresting questions with them (at least we think they are interesting). Everything we do is open source including work in progress, so you can get a sneek peek at upcoming articles on GitLab. Better yet, you can contribute by fixing typos, suggesting new topics or even performing some analyses yourself!
